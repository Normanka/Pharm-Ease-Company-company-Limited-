Mission

Deliver high-quality products and professional services through an interconnected network of NDA-accredited drugshops and pharmacies while investing into local drug R&D.

Vision

To be a leading, resilient, and ethically-driven pharmaceutical ecosystem integrating delivery with research and innovation.

Phase 1 (2026–2028)

Establish 30 NDA-accredited drugshops across major cities of Uganda and partner with local clinics and hospitals.

Phase 2 (2029–2031)

Establish 12 full-scale pharmacies and interconnect them with the drugshop network for continuous service delivery.

Phase 3

Build a Drug Research & Design Center and expand regionally the retail arm, focusing on local innovation.
